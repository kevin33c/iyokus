What do Iyokus offer?

Iyokus stands out from other e-commerce platforms in that it allows a buyer to buy a product by bidding a price that he or she are willing to pay and receive instant feedback.

Iyokus offers you the following advantages:

Decide your own price. Iyokus give you control and initiative over prices. If you are not willing to pay for the listed price, you always have the option to bid for a lower price1.
Instant feedback. There will be no more time wasted in haggling or waiting. Each bid is assessed and responded automatically within second.
Counter-offer system. If your initial bid is rejected, and when Iyokus finds it appropriate, you will get a counter-offer. You can accept it or reject it without any commitment.

1To ensure that the bids submitted are serious bids, Iyokus operates a "lock-out period". In other words, if a bid failed, you'll be blocked from bidding for the same product during a period of time. The lock-out period is defined by seller of the product.

Delivery

Orders are delivered safely and through the most cost efficient option

Receive your order

Iyokus offers two delivery options, "Meetup" delivery and "Postal" delivery. Currently, the postal delivery option is only available for business/professional sellers.

Meetup delivery: Once Iyokus confirms your order, the buyer will contact you within 2 days to agree on a meeting point and time to deliver the item. No additional cost with Meetup delivery.
Postal delivery1: The order will be delivered to an address of your choosing. Shipping costs2 bear by the buyer.
Meetup delivery
1 You will receive a Payment Code after completing the payment of the order.
2 The seller will contact you, via Iyokus Messenger, to agree a meeting location and time.
3 Meet up with the seller and receive the product.
4 Give the seller the Payment Code to release the payment and complete the order.
Postal delivery
1 Enter you desired delivery address before check out and complete the order.
2 Iyokus will notify you when your order is dispatched. Normally, orders are dispatched within 2 working days.
3 Receive1 and examine the product.
4 Rate the seller.

1 Delivery time can vary significantly depending on the origin of the product. A domestic product (i.e. UK/EU) usually take 3-5 working days to deliver. However, an international product can take up to 3-5 weeks. You'll find the delivery option and estimated time on each product page. Please review the delivery option before placing an order.

2 Shipping costs are different for each product. You will find the total shipping cost at check out or on the product page.

How to buy on Iyokus

How do Iyokus protect me if I do not receive my order or the product received is not as described?

If the item is new, you are entitled to return the item to the seller within 14 days without having to provide a reason. If you want to return an item, kindly notify us using your "My Orders" section.

If the item is used and you received it via Meetup1 delivery, you will have 24 hours to request a refund if the received product does not meet the criteria and description provided by the seller.

1 To complete the order, you will need to give the payment code to the seller. The payment code is used by Iyokus as a proof that the buyer is satisfied with the received item. Therefore, before only give the code to the seller if you are satisfied with the item.
