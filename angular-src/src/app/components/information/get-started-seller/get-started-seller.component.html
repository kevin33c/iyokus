What do Iyokus offer?

Unlike others e-commerce sites, with Iyokus you sell with a range of prices instead of a fixed price. With a fixed price you can only sell to those willing to pay the list price or above while with range pricing you can access to a whole range of willing buyers each with their own willingness to pay, thus maximizing your chance of sales.

Iyokus offers you the following advantages:

Dynamic and personalize prices. Range pricing ensures that each buyer pay what they are willing to pay while remaining satisfactory for you.
Automated bidding responses. There will be no more price haggling or waiting. Each bid is assessed and responded automatically and instantaneously.
Lock-out period. In order to ensure that you receive serious bids, buyers are blocked from bidding for the same product if his or her bid fails. You decide the duration of the lock-out period for each of your products.
Sell more and faster. Iyokus will connect you with more willing buyers.
Offer hidden discounts for your products With the range pricing, you can offer discounts without lowering the list price.

Delivery

Learn our different delivery methods

Deliver your order

Iyokus offers two delivery options, "Meetup" delivery and "Postal" delivery. Currently, the postal delivery option is only available for business/professional sellers.

Meetup delivery: Once you receive your order request, contact the buyer within 2 days, using Iyokus Messenger, to agree on a meeting point and time to deliver the item.
Postal delivery1: Dispatch the order within 2 working days to an address of the buyer's choosing. Shipping costs2 bear by the buyer.

1You decide the shipping costs. We expect 3-5 days delivery time for a domestically (UK & EU) sourced product and 3-5 weeks delivery time for an internationally sourced product.

Meetup delivery
1 Buyers will receive a Payment Code upon completing the payment and the order.
2 Contact the buyer, via Iyokus Messenger, to arrange a Meetup location and time for delivery.
3 Deliver the product and receive the Payment Code from the buyer.
4 Enter the Payment Code in the order's page to complete the order and receive the payment.
Postal delivery
1 Receive email with order request and delivery details incl. name and address.
2 Dispatch the order within 2 working days.
3 In the order's page, change the status of the order to "Dispatched" and enter the necessary details (i.e. delivery company and tracking code).
4 Receive the payment within 15 days after delivery completion.

When will I get the money after selling a product?
To save processing fees in case of refund, we'll deposit the payout due to you for each sale after the refund period expires, i.e. 14 days for new products and 24 hours for used products. In addition, it may take up from 1-3 days for the payout to appear on your account.

Why Iyokus charges a fee?
Iyokus charges fee to cover the payment processing charges by our payment provider . Additionally, we also charge a small fee to cover operational expenses and to improve our services.
